Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

+
Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a custom student management system may take 2-3 months.

What is your payment structure?

+
We typically work with a 30% upfront deposit, 40% at project midpoint, and 30% upon completion.

Do you provide ongoing support?

+
Yes! All projects include a support period, and we offer ongoing maintenance packages.

Can you work with non-profit budgets?

+
Absolutely. As a cooperative focused on social justice, we offer special pricing for non-profits and community organizations.
